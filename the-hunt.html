Concept and Objectives

The Hunt is a multiplayer game where players take on the roles of either a skilled assassin or a team of elite cops, each with unique abilities and objectives. Emphasising stealth, strategy, and teamwork, the game creates an adrenaline-pumping experience as players work to chase or evade targets in a high-stakes environment.

Design Elements

The Hunt features dynamic gameplay elements including leaderboards, interactable items, and an immersive audio system. Using LootLocker, players' scores for kills, damage, and deaths are tracked, fostering a competitive environment. Realistic audio cues and a strategic health system add depth to gameplay, enhancing the experience whether players are evading capture or pursuing their target.

Programming and Implementation

The Hunt was developed using Photon Unity Networking (PUN) for seamless multiplayer support, with additional backend services through LootLocker. Key scripts handle game mechanics, such as the PlayerStats and HealthBar systems, custom leaderboard integration, and synchronised audio feedback. RPC methods ensure consistent gameplay across clients, making the multiplayer experience smooth and engaging.
